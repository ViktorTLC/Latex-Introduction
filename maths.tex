\section{Mathematics}

Dollar signs are used to type mathematics:
$e^{i\pi}+1=0$.

Display mathematics:
$$
e^{i\pi}+1=0.
$$   \cite{9783639174304}

% the align environment (package: amsmath, amssymb)
Aligned mathematics:
\begin{align}
    e^{i\pi}&=\cos(\pi)+i\sin(\pi)\\
    &= -1 % "&" is used for alignment.
\end{align}

Aligned mathematics w/o number:
\begin{align*}
    e^{i\pi}&=\cos(\pi)+i\sin(\pi)\\
    &= -1 % "&" is used for alignment.    
\end{align*}
